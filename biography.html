Biography

Ricardo Wagner is a Dominican multidisciplinary visual artist based in Santo Domingo. His work spans painting, drawing, watercolor, photography and mixed media, developing a visual language rooted in abstraction and informalism. His artistic discourse explores creation, existence, and humanity’s relationship with nature, moving between the sacred and the terrestrial, the infinite and the intimate. Through abstraction, his paintings investigate the origin of things, the evolution of human consciousness, and the mystery of the universe.
