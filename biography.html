Biography

⌕

Ricardo Wagner is a Dominican multidisciplinary visual artist based in Santo Domingo whose practice moves between painting, drawing, watercolor, photography and mixed media.

Originally trained in advertising and communication, his work developed toward abstraction and informalism, exploring origin, existence and the relationship between humanity and nature.

He is founder of HD Graph Estudio Gráfico and Bremen Printing, experiences that shaped his technical and visual understanding of image construction.

His work has received recognition and awards, and has been featured in media such as Hoy, El Caribe, Diario Libre, Listín Diario and Noticias SIN.

Statement

My work explores existence through abstraction. Painting becomes a space where gesture, matter and intuition create a dialogue between the visible and the invisible.

I investigate the tension between the sacred and the earthly, the finite and the infinite, seeking to create contemplative experiences rather than representations.

Each painting develops through a balance between control and spontaneity, allowing the work to reveal its own internal structure and emotional resonance.

Ultimately, my intention is to invite reflection — to create a space where viewers encounter silence, presence and their own interpretation.
