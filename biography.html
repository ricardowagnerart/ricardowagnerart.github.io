Biography

Ricardo Wagner is a Dominican multidisciplinary visual artist whose practice explores abstraction, existence, and the relationship between humanity and nature.

Working across painting, drawing, watercolor, photography, and mixed media, his artistic language reflects a philosophical inquiry into origin, spirituality, and the infinite.

His work has been recognized in multiple exhibitions and awards, establishing a strong presence within contemporary Caribbean visual arts.

Through an abstract and expressive visual discourse, Wagner seeks to translate the mystical and the earthly into a unified pictorial experience.
